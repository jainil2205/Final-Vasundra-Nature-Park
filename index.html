About Vasundhara Farm Stay

Nestled in the heart of nature, our farm stay offers an authentic escape from city life, where sustainable farming meets comfortable hospitality.

Our Story

Founded in 1995 on 500 acres of pristine farmland, Vasundhara Farm Stay has been a pioneer in sustainable agriculture and eco-tourism. We combine traditional farming practices with modern comfort to create an unforgettable experience.

Lion Safari Experience

Embark on a guided safari through our 50-acre lion sanctuary, home to rescued and rehabilitated lions. Our expert guides provide fascinating insights into lion behavior and conservation efforts.

₹2,500 per person

Feeding Experience

Witness the power and majesty of lions during their feeding time. This supervised experience offers a unique opportunity to observe natural hunting instincts and social dynamics.

₹1,800 per person

Photography Workshop

Capture stunning wildlife photographs with guidance from professional photographers. Learn techniques for wildlife photography while documenting these magnificent creatures.

₹3,200 per person

Conservation Mission

Our lion sanctuary is part of a broader conservation initiative to protect and rehabilitate big cats. We work closely with wildlife authorities to provide a safe haven for lions that cannot be released into the wild.

  • 24/7 veterinary care and monitoring
  • Spacious natural habitat recreation
  • Educational programs for visitors
  • Research and breeding programs
